Seat Post

The seat post is an integral part of a bicycle that allows for the adjustment of the saddle height according to the rider's preference and comfort. To secure the seat post in place, a seat post clamp is used.

The clamp is positioned at the top of the seat tube and tightened to hold the seat post firmly, preventing it from slipping or moving during cycling. Proper adjustment and secure fastening of the seat post are essential for a stable and comfortable riding experience.
