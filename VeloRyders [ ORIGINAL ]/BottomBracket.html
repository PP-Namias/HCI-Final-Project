Bottom Bracket

The bottom bracket is a key part of a bicycle that allows the crank arms to rotate. It consists of a spindle, bearings, and fits into the bottom bracket shell of the frame. The spindle connects to the crankset, while the bearings enable smooth rotation.

The bottom bracket supports the crankset and ensures efficient power transfer. Proper maintenance is important for its smooth operation. Overall, the bottom bracket is crucial for the bike's performance and connects the crankset to the frame.
