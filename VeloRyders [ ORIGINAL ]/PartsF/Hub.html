Rim

The hub is a crucial part of a bicycle wheel, composed of the hub shell, bearings, and axle. The hub shell serves as the outer structure where the spokes attach.

The axle, which extends through the hub, connects the wheel to the bicycle frame. Bearings are situated between the axle and hub shell, enabling smooth rotation of the hub. Collectively, these components allow the wheel to rotate efficiently while providing stability and support to the bicycle.
