Fender

Fenders, also known as mudguards, are protective components installed on bicycles to prevent mud, water, and debris from being thrown up by the wheels. They help keep the rider and the bike cleaner, particularly in wet or muddy conditions.


Fenders come in different sizes and styles to fit various bike types and wheel sizes. They are practical accessories that enhance riding comfort and maintain a cleaner riding experience.
