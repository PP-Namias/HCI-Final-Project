Tire

The tire is an essential part of the bicycle that connects the wheel rim to the road. It is typically made of rubber and has a tread for improved traction. The tire encompasses an air-filled inner tube, which provides suspension and absorbs shocks. The tire, along with the inner tube, plays a crucial role in ensuring a comfortable and controlled ride by enhancing grip, cushioning, and overall performance.
