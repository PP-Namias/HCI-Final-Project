Cassette

The cassette is a series of sprockets or cogs attached to the hub of the back wheel. These cogs comprise the bike's back gears. Single-speed bikes usually have only one cog on the cassette, whereas geared bikes might have numerous gears.

The cassette enables the rider to vary the resistance and speed of the bike by moving the chain onto different cogs. The size and arrangement of cogs on the cassette can change, providing a variety of gear ratios to suit varied riding situations and terrains.
