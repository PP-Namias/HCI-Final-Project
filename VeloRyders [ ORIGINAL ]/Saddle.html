Saddle

The saddle is an essential component of a bike where the rider sits. It comes in various shapes and sizes to accommodate different preferences and riding styles. The saddle features saddle rails on its underside, which are used to attach it to the seat post using a saddle clamp.

The saddle plays a crucial role in providing comfort and support during cycling by distributing the rider's weight and reducing pressure points. Choosing the right saddle that suits your body type and riding needs is important for an enjoyable and comfortable biking experience.
